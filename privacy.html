Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Who we are

Frameshift ApS ("Frameshift") is a biostatistics consultancy registered in Denmark (CVR 44531410). Our website address is https://www.frameshift.dk.

What data we collect

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools, and we do not collect personal data automatically. Like most web hosts, our hosting provider (GitHub, Inc.) may log technical data such as visitor IP addresses for security and operational purposes; see GitHub's data collection notes. If you switch the site to dark mode, that preference is stored in your own browser and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Contact form

The contact form on this website is processed by Formspree, Inc., a form-handling service based in the United States. When you submit the form, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to Formspree, forwarded to us by email, and stored by Formspree until we delete them. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and do not share it with anyone else. Because Formspree operates from the United States, submitting the form involves a transfer of your data outside the EU/EEA, governed by Formspree's data processing terms; see Formspree's privacy policy.

If you prefer not to use the form, you can email us directly at info@frameshift.dk. In either case, please do not include confidential, sensitive, or patient-level information in an initial enquiry.

External services

The only third-party service used by this website is Formspree, which processes contact-form submissions as described above. All fonts, scripts, and other assets are self-hosted. No other data is shared with external providers through your use of this website.

Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. These rights apply to any data you provide via the contact form or by email.

To exercise your rights or ask questions about our data practices, contact us at info@frameshift.dk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
