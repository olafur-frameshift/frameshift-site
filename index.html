How we help

Shaping the question and analysis

We work with teams that have data and broad objectives, and want to shape clear analytical paths. That often means clarifying what can credibly be supported and which analyses are worth pursuing.

Statistical planning and trial analysis

Clear, decision-ready SAPs, trial analyses, and reporting that defines estimands, endpoints, population, and strategy from the outset. Costs tend to surface from ambiguity.

Registry and real-world evidence

Design and execution of observational studies using health registers and other real-world data. Credible results rely on attention to bias, timing, and comparability.

NEJM Evidence 2024

Intact Fish Skin Graft to Treat Deep Diabetic Foot Ulcers

Randomised trial across 255 patients testing fish skin grafts against standard care for deep diabetic foot ulcers. Fish skin achieved wound closure in 44% versus 26% at 16 weeks.

British Journal of Cancer 2024

Infections and Their Prognostic Significance Before Diagnosis of CLL, Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma, or Multiple Myeloma

Danish registry study of 30,000 patients showing infection burden is elevated up to 15 years before diagnosis of B-cell malignancies, with implications for early immune dysfunction and prognosis.

Epidemiological and Genetic Evidence for Shared Mechanisms Between Migraine and Pre-eclampsia

Nationwide Danish cohort and genetic risk score study finding shared aetiological pathways between migraine and pre-eclampsia, with implications for cardiovascular risk stratification.
